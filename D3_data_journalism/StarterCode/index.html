Correlations Discovered Between Poverty and Lack of Physical Activity

We are exploring the correlations between social class demographics and behavioral risk factors being lack of physical activity.

The data collected was from the American Community Survey (ACS) and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) during the year of 2014. Each marker indicates an individual state in the United States. You can click on the markers to explore the data!

The calculated correlation between Poverty and Physical Activity is -0.67
